Detailed Description

Structure representing a polynom.

The degree of the polynom is stored in an mpz_t number and the coefficient of the polynom are stored in a linked list.

The index of the most/least significant bit of the mpz_t number is the degree of coefficient of the highest/lowest degree.
The first/last element of the linked list is the coefficient of highest/lowest degree.

For instance, the polynom 2 + x + x^3 is stored as:

  • index_coeff = 11_{10} = 1011_{2}
  • coeff = [2, 1, 1]
Parameters
index_coeffThe mpz_t number representing the degree of the coefficients of the polynom.
coeffThe linked list that stores the coefficients of the polynom.
